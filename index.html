Best Starter Pokémon Ranked

Your ultimate guide to the cutest starter Pokémon!

1Pikachu

Pikachu is a popular and iconic Pokémon known for its yellow fur, pointy ears, and red cheeks that store electricity. It is an Electric-type Pokémon and the mascot of the Pokémon franchise. Friendly, energetic, and loyal, Pikachu is most famously seen as the companion of Ash Ketchum in the Pokémon anime. It can generate powerful electric attacks like "Thunderbolt" and "Electro Ball," making it both cute and strong. Pikachu's charm and popularity have made it a global symbol of Pokémon.

2Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon known for the flame that burns at the tip of its tail, which reflects its health and mood. It resembles a small orange dinosaur or lizard and evolves into Charmeleon and then the powerful Charizard. Charmander is brave and determined, often seen as a loyal and friendly companion. It can use fire-based attacks like “Ember” and “Flamethrower.” Introduced as one of the original starter Pokémon, Charmander is loved for its fiery spirit and strong bond with its trainer.

3Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon known for the plant bulb on its back, which grows into a large flower as it evolves. It has a calm and gentle nature, making it a dependable companion. Bulbasaur can use powerful moves like "Vine Whip" and "Razor Leaf," using the energy stored in its bulb. As one of the original starter Pokémon, it evolves into Ivysaur and then Venusaur. Bulbasaur is admired for its balanced abilities and strong connection with nature.

4Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon that resembles a small blue turtle with a curled tail and a hard shell for protection. It is known for its playful and brave personality, often staying calm even in tough situations. Squirtle can shoot powerful streams of water from its mouth using moves like “Water Gun” and “Hydro Pump.” As it evolves, it becomes Wartortle and then the strong Blastoise. Squirtle is one of the original starter Pokémon and is loved for its loyalty, courage, and cool appearance.

5Tepig

Tepig is a Fire-type Pokémon that looks like a small, pig-like creature with an orange and black body and a curly tail. It is known for its cheerful and energetic nature. Tepig can shoot fire from its snout and uses moves like “Ember” and “Flame Charge” to battle opponents. It evolves into Pignite and then into the powerful Emboar. As a starter Pokémon from the Unova region, Tepig is loved for its determination, cuteness, and fiery fighting spirit.
